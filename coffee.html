While normalized in society today, caffeine still remains a drug, and like any drug, may come with some serious side effects. This module helps you track your caffeine intake so you can figure out the optimal amount to consume without experiencing the adverse side effects of too much caffeine.

For amount, enter how much of the appropriate unit you consumed at that time for that item:
Caffeinated Soda: 12oz
Tea, Coffee: 8oz
Energy Drink: 16oz
Espresso: 1 shot
Chocolate: 1oz

For espresso based drinks (lattes, capuccinos, etc.), enter 2 shots of espresso for a small, 3 for a large

Alternatively, if your caffeinated consumable is not listed as an option, but you know the amount of caffeine you consumed from it in mg, you can add it as a custom item.
